About the Designer & Founder

Michał Jarociński

One of few professional type designers in Poland, graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw. From 2007, he has been working under the name Dada Studio, making websites and fonts. Michał is the originator of the Capitalics Warsaw Type Foundry project. He supervises the whole project and cares about the high level of typefaces.
